People to People Programs

Experience authentic Cuba through meaningful cultural exchanges and direct interaction with Cuban people across all walks of life.

Cultural Exchange at Its Best

Our People to People programs are designed to promote genuine interaction between American visitors and the Cuban people. These programs focus on educational and cultural exchanges that provide insight into Cuban society, history, and daily life.

Participants engage with Cuban professionals, artists, entrepreneurs, educators, and community leaders to gain a deeper understanding of contemporary Cuban culture and the challenges and opportunities facing the island today.

What Makes Our Programs Special:

  • Direct interaction with Cuban families and communities
  • Visits to local schools, healthcare facilities, and cooperatives
  • Meetings with Cuban artists, musicians, and writers
  • Participation in community projects and cultural activities

Program Categories

Educational Exchange

Visit Cuban schools and universities, meet with educators and students, and participate in academic discussions about Cuban education system and reforms.

Arts & Culture

Engage with Cuban artists, musicians, and cultural practitioners. Visit studios, attend performances, and participate in cultural workshops.

Community Development

Learn about grassroots initiatives, cooperative movements, and community-based projects that are transforming Cuban neighborhoods.

Professional Exchange

Connect with Cuban professionals in your field, visit workplaces, and discuss industry practices and innovations.
